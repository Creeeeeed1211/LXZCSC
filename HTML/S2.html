British Soldier

Main Weapon: Lee-Enfield No. 4 Rifle

Helmet: The MkII steel helmet.

Bayonet: British soldiers were often equipped with a bayonet that could be attached to their rifle for close combat.

Grenades: British soldiers were issued with a variety of grenades, including the No. 36 Mills Bomb and the No. 69 anti-tank grenade.
